2. END-TO-END WORKFLOW GUIDE
---------------------------------------------

Follow this sequence to test the full lifecycle of a request:

STEP 1: CREATE REQUEST (PMO)
- Login: pmo@gov.in
- Action: Click "New Request".
- Fill: Title (e.g., "School Inspection"), Description, Due Date.
- Select: State: "Delhi", Division: "Education".
- Submit.
- Status: Pending CEO Review

STEP 2: APPROVE REQUEST (CEO)
- Login: ceo.niti@gov.in
- Action: You will see the request in your dashboard. Click "View Details".
- Action: Click "Approve & Forward".
- Status: Pending State Advisor

STEP 3: FORWARD TO STATE TEAM (State Advisor)
- Login: harsh@niti.gov.in
- Action: View Details -> "Approve & Forward".
- Status: Pending State YP

STEP 4: FANOUT TO DIVISIONS (State YP)
- Login: harsh2@niti.gov.in
- Action: View Details -> "Approve & Fanout to Divisions".
- Status: Pending Division HOD (The request is now sent to the Education division).

STEP 5: APPROVE FOR FIELD WORK (Division HOD)
- Login: harsh3@niti.gov.in
- Action: View Details -> "Approve & Forward to Division YP".
- Status: Pending Division YP

STEP 6: SUBMIT DATA (Division YP)
- Login: harsh4@niti.gov.in
- Action: View Details. You will see a "Submit Information" panel.
- Action: Fill out the form (enter text, mock data). Click "Submit".
- Status: Pending Division HOD Approval

STEP 7: REVIEW SUBMISSION (Division HOD)
- Login: harsh3@niti.gov.in
- Action: View Details. You will see the submitted data.
- Action: Click "Approve Form & Forward to State YP".

STEP 8: MERGE & FINALIZE (State YP)
- Login: harsh2@niti.gov.in
- Action: View Details. You will see submissions from divisions.
- Action: Click "Merge & Forward to State Advisor".

STEP 9: FINAL REVIEW (State Advisor)
- Login: harsh@niti.gov.in
- Action: View Details -> "Approve & Close".
- Status: Completed

STEP 10: VIEW FINAL REPORT (PMO/CEO)
- Login: pmo@gov.in or ceo.niti@gov.in
- Action: View Details. You can now see the completed request with all the data.
